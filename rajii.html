Artificial Intelligence in Cybersecurity

Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing the cybersecurity landscape by enabling systems to detect, prevent, and respond to threats more efficiently than ever before.

Applications of AI in Cybersecurity

  • Threat detection and response
  • Behavioral analytics to detect anomalies
  • Fraud prevention in financial transactions
  • Automated malware analysis
  • Phishing detection and email security

Conclusion

AI is a powerful tool in defending against the growing number and complexity of cyber threats. However, it also introduces new challenges, such as adversarial AI, that cybersecurity experts must address.
